# Monthly Monte Carlo Simulator

The purpose is to provide a Monte Carlo simulator for a price over the course of a number of years.

## Inputs

* Starting Price
* Monthly Volatility
* Mean Price Change
* Price Floor
* Price Ceiling
* Beginning Date
* End Date

## Outputs
The following should be output for each month:
* Mean Price
* Median Price
* Standard Deviation
* Percentiles
	* 5%
	* 10%
	* 25%
	* 50%
	* 75%
	* 90%
	* 95*
